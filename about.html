Where It All Began

I had a strong interest in dance from a young age, but turning that passion into something real needed a start.

In the beginning, my parents supported me financially and emotionally. That support became the foundation for everything that followed.

What started small, with limited resources and big uncertainty, slowly grew into a journey filled with learning, failures, growth, and resilience.

This journey is not just about success — it’s about never giving up, no matter how many times things fall apart.

— Founder
2014

The Foundation

Began professional acting classes and structured dance training. Completed instructor training and built a strong artistic base rooted in discipline and performance.

2016

From Student to Mentor

Transitioned into teaching, training students in the same institute where the journey began — marking the shift from learner to leader.

2017

First Studio Launch

Co-founded “Spirit Radiant Dance Academy” in Kumaraswamy Layout (Aug 21), later rebranded as “Shaivam – The School of Dance and Arts.” Delivered school projects and the team’s first major stage performance in December.

2018

Independent Expansion

Navigated structural transitions and continued independently. Celebrated the first anniversary, initiated a web series muhurtha, expanded classes across multiple locations, and executed numerous stage performances and contract projects.

2019

International Qualification

Qualified for finals of an international dance competition in Portugal after months of intensive preparation. Though the finals couldn’t be executed, the experience reshaped the long-term vision. Began expanding into events and film-related execution.

2020

Lockdown & The Vision

Studio operations paused during lockdown. Instead of stopping, conceptualized “Bee Infinity Entertainments” with a structured multi-vertical vision: Dance, Events, Film & Post Production.

2022

Bee Infinity Groups Formed

Re-established under the name Bee Infinity Groups with Bharathi as Co-Founder. Structured verticals were formed: Bee Infinity Dance Crew, Bee Infinity Events, and Bee Infinity Entertainments. Classes restarted from a home studio setup while events expanded steadily.

2023

Infrastructure Growth

Built an in-house dubbing studio entirely by hand. Expanded post-production capabilities and evolved Bee Infinity Entertainments into B.I.G Cinemaz.

2024

Strategic Recalibration

Relocated into a larger studio partnership. After operational restructuring, continued independently with renewed clarity and stronger leadership focus.

2025

Resilience & Stability

Focused on strengthening core operations while navigating personal and financial challenges. Maintained all vertical services with discipline and long-term clarity.

2026

Structured Expansion

Introduced new school services, initiated film acquisition under B.I.G Cinemaz, and launched BOTD initiatives — executing growth with strategic direction and sustainability.

B.I.G Cinemaz

Film Production & Post Production

B.I.G Cinemaz provides complete filmmaking solutions from concept development to final delivery. Our services include pre-production, shoot execution, editing, DI, VFX, sound design, dubbing, posters, and subtitles.

Bee Infinity Events

Event Management

We manage corporate, educational, and family events with complete planning, execution, and documentation. Our event solutions include stage design, coordination, video coverage, and photography.

Bee Infinity Dance Crew

Dance & Choreography

Our dance crew specializes in school and college choreography, personal training, and performance choreography for events and celebrations. We focus on energy, precision, and visual impact.
